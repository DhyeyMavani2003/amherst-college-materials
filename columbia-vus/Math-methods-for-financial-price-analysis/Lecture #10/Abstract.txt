This is a presentation of the "DrawDown Measure in Portoflio Optimization" paper 
accepted for publication to International Journal of Theoretical and Applied 
Finance (IJTAF), published by the "World Scientific", to the Third World Congress 
of the Bachelier Finance Society, held in Chicago, USA in July 2004. 

In this presentation, we explain the advantages, properties and motivation behind 
the DrawDown Risk Measure for Portfolio Optimization are presented in several 
examples of market indices, and two practical portfolio selection problems: risk 
allocation for a Futures Trend-Following Systems Portfolio, and a selection of 
weights for a Long/Short Equity Portfolio.
